ABOUT US




Today in times of the Corona Virus pandemic, there is a gap between the existing information about businesses transmitted to the customers through the network and the real accurate information, in terms of the availability hours of the various businesses during the Corona Virus and the ability to manage customer's reception.

The "INFOPACITY" site is designed to make it easier for businesses and their customers during the times of Corona Virus.

- Business owners can update their customers about the business activity and its occupancy in real time, as well, business owners can manage the business properly and the business guidance from the Ministry of Health during the Corona epidemic.

- Business owners will be allowed to add details and additional description of the business (phone, fax, images).

- Customers who will be interested in up-to-date and relevant information (during the Corona epidemic) about the businesses registered in the system, can perform a search by the name of the business and get information.
